Multimodal Emotion Recognition

Late Fusion Analysis (Face + Audio + Text)

Abstract

This project presents a deep learning–based Multimodal Emotion Recognition system that integrates audio, video, and text to achieve emotion classification. Unlike unimodal approaches that struggle with noise, occlusion, and ambiguity, the proposed framework leverages three powerful pre-trained models—Wav2Vec2 for audio, MobileNetV2 for facial features, and BERT for text understanding—to extract rich and complementary features from each modality.
